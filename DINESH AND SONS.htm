Types of woods our company prefer to manufacture products.

  • Teak Wood
    • Features: Highly durable, water-resistant, termite-resistant, rich grain texture, and requires minimal maintenance.
      • Used for: Beds, chairs, doors, windows, outdoor furniture, and luxury wooden furniture.
  • Sheesham Wood (Indian Rosewood)
    • Features: Strong, durable, attractive grain patterns, termite-resistant, and takes polish well.
      • used for: Study tables, cabinets, beds, dining tables, and decorative wooden items.
  • Walnut Wood
    • Features: Strong, lightweight, shock-resistant, rich dark brown color, and has an elegant grain pattern.
      • Used for: High-end furniture, doors, cabinets, beds, and wooden carvings.
  • Cedar Wood
    • Features: Naturally insect and rot-resistant, aromatic scent, lightweight yet durable, and has a smooth texture.
      • Used for: Wardrobes, chests, outdoor furniture, and paneling.
